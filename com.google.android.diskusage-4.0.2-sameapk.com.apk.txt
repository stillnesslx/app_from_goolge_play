DiskUsage
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.diskusage

** OnePlus3 users, please ask the developers to fix their OS: http://goo.gl/r05VBc **

DiskUsage provides a way to find files and directories on storage card which consume a lot of space.

Displays diagram with directories/subdirectories proportional to their size.

Press MENU for actions.

Integrates with OI FileManager and Solid Explorer.

If program fails or gives you out of memory error message - please contact me. Thanks.
